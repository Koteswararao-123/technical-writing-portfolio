# User Login Guide


## 1. Overview  
This guide explains how users can log in to the application using their registered credentials. It is designed to help both new and existing users access the system easily.


## 2. Prerequisites  
Before logging in, make sure you have:
- A registered account  
- Valid username or email  
- Password  
- Stable internet connection  


## 3. Steps to Login  
### Step 1: Open the Login Page  
Open your web browser and navigate to the login page of the application.

### Step 2: Enter Credentials  
Enter your login details:
- Username / Email  
- Password  

### Step 3: Click Login  
Click on the **Login** button to proceed.

### Step 4: Access Dashboard  
If the credentials are correct, you will be redirected to your dashboard or home page.


## 4. Forgot Password  
If you forget your password:
1. Click on **Forgot Password**
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Create a new password and login again
 

## 5. Common Issues & Solutions  
### Issue: Invalid credentials  
**Solution:** Check your username/email and password carefully and try again.

### Issue: Page not loading  
**Solution:** Verify your internet connection and refresh the page.

### Issue: Account locked  
**Solution:** Wait for a few minutes before retrying or contact the support team for assistance.


## 6. Summary  
The login process is simple and secure. Always ensure you enter correct credentials to access your account without issues.
